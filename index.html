About

Nanditha is an architect and a creative thinker with more than three years of experience in designing artful and pragmatic structures. After completing her formal education in Architecture from the College of Engineering, Trivandrum, she worked in various innovative architectural studios in and around Kerala and Bangalore. Influenced by the likes of Geoffrey Bawa and Marina Tabassum, Nanditha shares a similar sensitive approach to design, blending ethnic avant-garde in to contemporary architecture.
